# FUMOCA Sound Assets

## Required sound files

Place your audio files (MP3 or OGG, keep under 2MB each) at these paths:

### Ambient tracks
sounds/ambient/showroom-exterior.mp3   — vehicle exterior ambience
sounds/ambient/vehicle-interior-idle.mp3 — car cabin interior
sounds/ambient/interior-ambience.mp3   — indoor property ambience
sounds/ambient/outdoor-residential.mp3 — outdoor neighbourhood sound
sounds/ambient/crowd-outdoor.mp3       — outdoor event crowd
sounds/ambient/studio-silence.mp3      — very subtle room tone for products
sounds/ambient/cinematic-drone.mp3     — low cinematic drone for scene reconstructions
sounds/ambient/subtle-texture.mp3      — minimal texture for historical images

### UI sounds (keep under 100KB each)
sounds/ui/portal-enter.mp3   — soft whoosh when entering interior
sounds/ui/portal-exit.mp3    — soft whoosh when exiting interior
sounds/ui/hotspot-tap.mp3    — gentle ping on hotspot tap
sounds/ui/ad-activate.mp3    — shimmer when interactive mode activates
sounds/ui/limit-soft.mp3     — very soft bump at scene boundary
sounds/ui/tour-advance.mp3   — soft chime on tour stop advance

## Free sound sources
- freesound.org (CC0 licence)
- pixabay.com/music (royalty free)
- zapsplat.com (free with attribution)
- soundbible.com (CC0)

## Tips
- Use MP3 at 128kbps for ambient tracks
- Use OGG at 96kbps for UI sounds (smaller, faster)
- Keep all sounds loopable if they will be used as ambient tracks
- Test at low volumes — these are background sounds, not foreground
